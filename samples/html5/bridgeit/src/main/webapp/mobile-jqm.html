About BridgeIt

Instant HTML5 Hybrids

BridgeIt Is Mobile

  • Access mobile device capabilities directly from your HTML5 applications.
  • Support major mobile device platforms, including Android and iOS, with Blackberry 10, and Windows Phone soon to come.

BridgeIt Is Flexible

  • Works with any HTML5/JavaScript-based development approach.
  • Works seamlessly with any standard mobile browser.

BridgeIt Is Easy

  • Add hybrid capabilities to your HTML5 application in 5 minutes or less. See how...
  • No native development, packaging, or app store publication required.

BridgeIt Is Cheap
