The tabset component provides controls for selecting which child contentPane component will be displayed. The tabset selection tabs can be shown above or below the child content. The tabset can also be made to fit the bounds of its parent.

   <mobi:tabset>
      <mobi:contentPane
        id="tab1" >
         ...
      </mobi:contentPane>
      <mobi:contentPane
        id="tab2" >
         ...
      </mobi:contentPane>
   </mobi:tabset>
