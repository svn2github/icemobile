Mobi components have been skinned to match the mobile platforms, but can also be easily extended to match custom corporate themes. The mobile platforms Android, iOS and Blackberry have been targeted for initial support. jQuery Mobile ThemeRoller is also supported and existing mobile ThemeRoller skins can be imported

To have a device theme automatically applied to your ICEmobile page, simply include the following component in the head of your page:

  <mobi:deviceResources />

The deviceResource component will automatically detect the current platform and load the corresponding CSS. Specific themes can also be used by using the 'theme' attribute and specifying one of the built-in themes, such as iPhone (iphone), iPad (ipad), Android Holo light and dark (android_light & android_dark), BlackBerry (bberry), BlackBerry 10 (bb10), and jQuery mobile (jqm).
